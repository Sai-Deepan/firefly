## Core Safety & Survival Features

1. **Instant Threat Awareness**

   * Real-time hazard status: *Clear / Warning / Danger*
   * Simple color-coded UI (green / amber / red)
   * Zero cognitive load

2. **Dynamic Evacuation Guidance**

   * Live navigation to **nearest safe exit**
   * Reroutes instantly if conditions change
   * Avoids congested paths automatically

3. **Emergency SOS (One-Tap)**

   * Sends:

     * User’s live location
     * Identity (optional)
     * Current hazard context
   * Triggers emergency services + trusted contacts

4. **Offline Fail-Safe Mode**

   * Works during:

     * Internet outage
     * Power failure
   * Uses cached maps + last known sensor data

---

## Environmental Awareness

5. **Air Quality & Smoke Alerts**

   * AQI, smoke density, toxic gas detection
   * Health-based thresholds (asthma, elderly, kids)

6. **Temperature & Heat Stress Alerts**

   * High heat / extreme cold warnings
   * Predictive alerts before conditions become lethal

7. **Hazard-Specific Instructions**

   * Fire → Stay low, avoid elevators
   * Gas leak → Ventilate, evacuate
   * Earthquake → Drop, cover, hold → then evacuate

---

## Crowd & Personal Context Intelligence

8. **Crowd Density Awareness**

   * Warns user if route ahead is congested
   * Suggests alternative exits proactively

9. **Indoor Location Tracking**

   * Room-level accuracy (Room 101, Staircase B)
   * No GPS dependency indoors

10. **Personal Risk Profile**

    * User can set:

      * Mobility limitations
      * Medical conditions
      * Preferred exits
    * Routing adapts accordingly

---

## Communication & Trust

11. **Multi-Channel Alerts**

    * App notification
    * SMS fallback
    * Audible alarms (even on silent)

12. **Live Status Sharing**

    * Share “I’m Safe / I Need Help” status
    * Optional family or group visibility

13. **Responder Awareness**

    * Shows when emergency responders are nearby
    * Confirms SOS was received (critical trust feature)

## Automation & Smart Integration

14. **Home / Building Automation Hooks**

    * Auto unlock emergency exits
    * Shut down gas lines
    * Turn on emergency lighting

15. **Wearable Integration (Optional)**

    * Smartwatch vibration alerts
    * Hands-free SOS trigger
